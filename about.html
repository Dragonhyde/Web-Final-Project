About Me

You might be wondering, “Another thrift store? What makes this one different?” Let me tell you.

Back in 2019, I found myself at a local thrift store in Calgary, searching for affordable clothes. What I stumbled upon was disheartening—shirts with holes, pants with stains, hoodies that had seen better days, and even coats and hats that were unrecognizable. I realized then that too many thrift stores prioritized quantity over quality, leaving shoppers to sift through items that should never have made it to the racks.

That day sparked a dream: to reimagine the thrift store experience. I wanted to create a space where affordability didn’t mean sacrificing quality—a place where people could find clothing that was clean, repaired, and ready to wear, no matter their budget.

Fast forward to today, and that dream has become a reality with It’s New Again. We’re not just another thrift store; we’re on a mission to set a new standard. Every item we sell is carefully inspected, cleaned, and repaired to be as close to new as possible. We believe everyone deserves access to clothing they can feel confident in, and we’re proud to offer it at prices that make sense for every wallet.

At It’s New Again, we’re here to show that second-hand can be second to none. Thank you for being part of this journey. Together, we’re making a difference—one quality piece at a time.
